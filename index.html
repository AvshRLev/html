Hey everybody, my name is Avshalom and one of my favorite things to do is make baskets from natural materials i collect around where i live.


I first started making baskets 18 years ago when i was doing an intructor course with a group called Shomrei Hagan that teaches skills that has to do with survival and earth living. One of the skills we learned was basket weaving using palm branches and i fell in love immediately and have been weaving ever since.




I spent many of my years as basket weaver teaching myself and learning from experience and trial and error but a few weavers inspired me along the way and gave me some important lessons i would not have learned without them. All in all i would say that it is good to learn by yourself and experiment but sometimes it really helps to learn from somebody more experienced.
